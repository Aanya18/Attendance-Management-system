How Our System Works

A simple and efficient process to manage attendance.

1. Register & Setup

Principals register the school. Teachers and students are added to the system.

2. Mark Attendance

Teachers easily mark daily attendance for their classes through an intuitive interface.

3. View Reports

Principals and teachers can view detailed attendance reports and analytics.

User Roles

Our system supports different roles with specific permissions

Teacher

  • Add and manage students
  • Mark daily attendance
  • View attendance records for their students
  • Upload attendance record images
  • Generate student-specific reports

Principal

  • Manage teachers and other principals
  • View all students and attendance records
  • Access historical attendance data
  • Verify attendance through uploaded images
  • Generate and export comprehensive reports
